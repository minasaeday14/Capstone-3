# Capstone 3 Working Checklist

## Core Build Order
1. Import the provided sales data into Power BI.
2. Review field names, data types, null values, and duplicates.
3. Clean product, category, state, and date fields.
4. Confirm how to identify:
   - region
   - state
   - book vs textbook
   - author
   - sales amount
5. Build or validate a proper date table if needed.
6. Create measures for:
   - total sales
   - sales YTD
   - average order value
   - units sold
7. Build the required visuals.
8. Add descriptive page headers and consistent formatting.
9. Review for presentation readiness.
10. Publish to Power BI Service and capture the link.

## Required Visuals
- Line chart: sales trend across full date range
- Bar or column chart: sales by category
- Pie or donut chart: relative sales by state in your region
- Top-selling books list with author names, excluding textbooks

## Questions To Resolve Once Data Is Added
- What region are we assigned?
- Which field defines a textbook versus general audience book?
- Is sales measured as revenue, sales amount, or net sales?
- Are returns or refunds included?
- Do online and store sales need to be compared separately?

## Suggested Extra Insights
- Top 5 states in region by revenue
- Best and worst performing months
- Year-over-year sales growth
- Sales split by channel
- Top authors by revenue
